In 2023, our founder Sarah was a full-time travel influencer managing over 50,000 photos and videos across multiple platforms. She spent 15+ hours every week just organizing content, finding the right shots, and manually engaging with followers.

That frustration sparked an idea: what if AI could handle all the tedious parts of being a creator, so you could focus purely on your craft?
